Introduction

The following rules and regulations are established to ensure the safety, security, and well-being of all hostel residents. Every student is expected to strictly adhere to these rules during their stay in the hostel.

General Conduct

  • Residents must maintain discipline and decorum within the hostel premises.
  • Respect for hostel staff, wardens, and fellow students is mandatory.
  • No form of ragging, bullying, or harassment will be tolerated.
  • Silence hours must be observed from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM daily.

Room & Property Care

  • Residents are responsible for the cleanliness of their rooms.
  • Any damage to hostel property must be reported immediately.
  • Students will bear the cost of intentional damage to furniture, walls, or electrical appliances.

Mess & Dining Hall

  • Mess timings must be strictly followed.
  • Wasting food is strictly prohibited.
  • Residents are not allowed to take utensils or food outside the dining hall.

Safety & Security

  • Residents must carry their hostel ID cards at all times.
  • No outsider is allowed without prior permission from the warden.
  • Possession or consumption of alcohol, drugs, or smoking is strictly banned.

Internet & Gadgets

  • Internet usage should be strictly for educational purposes.
  • Playing loud music or causing disturbance to others is not allowed.
  • Electrical appliances like heaters and irons are not permitted without approval.

Disciplinary Action

  • Violation of hostel rules will invite strict disciplinary action.
  • Serious violations may result in expulsion from the hostel.
  • The decision of the hostel warden/management will be final.
